### Template Preview
Clicking a template shows:
- Description of the method
- Visual preview of the timeline structure
- List of chapters/beats with descriptions
- "Apply to Book" button

### What a Template Creates
- Chapters with names and descriptions from the method
- A "Main Plot" plotline
- Placeholder scenes with guidance text (e.g., "This is the Inciting Incident вҖ” something disrupts your protagonist's normal world")

### Custom Templates
- "Save as Template" on any book
- Saves: chapter structure, plotline structure, scene scaffolding (titles only)
- Does NOT save: actual scene content, character data, Google Doc links

### Series Timeline
- A special timeline view that spans all books
- Each book is a "super-chapter" containing its own chapters
- Useful for tracking series-level arcs (e.g., a villain's plan across 3 books)

### Shared Story Bible
- Characters, places, notes, and tags are project-scoped
- In a series, they're shared across all books
- "Appears In" on a character shows scenes across all books
- Can filter by book within the story bible views
